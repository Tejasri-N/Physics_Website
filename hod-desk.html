Prof. Manish K. Niranjan
HoD Profile

It is with great pride that I share the recent developments and academic progress in the Department of Physics at IIT Hyderabad. Our department continues to evolve as a hub of advanced research, interdisciplinary learning, and high-quality teaching.

In line with emerging national priorities and technological frontiers, we have recently introduced two new academic programs: the M.Tech. in Quantum and Solid State Devices (QSD) and the M.Sc. in Medical Physics. The M.Tech. in QSD is designed to equip students with strong foundations in quantum technologies and semiconductor device physics, preparing them to contribute meaningfully to the growing fields of quantum computing, nanoelectronics, and materials science. The M.Sc. in Medical Physics, developed with clinical relevance and academic rigor, aims to produce skilled professionals who can contribute to the healthcare sector, particularly in medical imaging, radiation therapy, and diagnostic technologies.

Our department is home to a dynamic team of faculty members and researchers engaged in a wide range of frontier areas in physics. We take pride in our growing infrastructure, collaborative research environment, and the enthusiastic participation of students in both theoretical and experimental domains.

We welcome aspiring students, researchers, and industry collaborators to connect with us and be part of our journey in pushing the frontiers of physics.
