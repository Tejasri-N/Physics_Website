About B.Tech in Physics

The B.Tech program in Physics at IIT Hyderabad is designed to provide students with a strong foundation in fundamental and applied physics. The curriculum balances theoretical courses with hands-on laboratory experience, encouraging analytical thinking and problem-solving skills.

  • Comprehensive core courses in classical and modern physics
  • Electives in interdisciplinary and emerging areas
  • Research opportunities in state-of-the-art laboratories
  • Industry internships and collaborative projects
  • Preparation for careers in research, technology, and higher studies
